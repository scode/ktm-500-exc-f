# Torque Specifications

This is unofficial. Always verify against your official KTM manual before tightening anything.

## From the repair manual

These are from the "repair" manual which is distinct from the "owner's" manual.

| Fastener                     | Torque (Nm) | Threadlocker? | Description                                                     |
| ---------------------------- | ----------- | ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Handlebar mount screws (M10) | 40          | Loctite 243   | Screws fastening the handlebar support to the top triple clamp. |
| Handlebar clamp screws (M8)  | 20          | Not specified | Screws fastening the handlebar clamp to the handlebar support.  |
| Rear sprocket bolts (M8)     | 35          | Loctite 2701  | Nuts+bolts holding rear sprocket to the wheel.                  |
